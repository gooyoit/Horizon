## [Moonshot AI Introduces Attention Residuals for Efficient Transformers](https://github.com/MoonshotAI/Attention-Residuals/blob/master/Attention_Residuals.pdf) ⭐️ 9.0/10

Moonshot AI has introduced Attention Residuals (AttnRes), a novel modification to the Transformer architecture that replaces fixed residual connections with learnable, input-dependent attention over previous layer outputs. Applied to their 48B-parameter Kimi Linear model, it achieves 1.25x training efficiency and improved performance on reasoning, math, and coding benchmarks. This innovation addresses a long-standing limitation in deep Transformer models—information dilution across layers—potentially enabling more efficient scaling of large language models without sacrificing performance. It could influence future LLM architectures by rethinking how information flows through depth. Attention Residuals adds less than 4% training overhead and increases inference latency by no more than 2%. It specifically mitigates the 'PreNorm dilution' problem by improving gradient flow and allowing earlier layers to retain meaningful influence in deeper networks.

telegram · zaihuapd · Mar 16, 09:05

**Background**: Standard Transformers use fixed residual connections that uniformly add each layer’s output to a running hidden state. In PreNorm variants—common in modern LLMs—this causes hidden states to grow unboundedly with depth, diluting individual layer contributions. This 'PreNorm dilution' limits effective information propagation in very deep models. Attention Residuals reinterprets depth as a sequence dimension, applying softmax attention across layers to dynamically weight their importance.
